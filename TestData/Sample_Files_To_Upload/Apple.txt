APPLE AS A FRUIT:

Apples are delicious fruits that come in red, yellow, and green varieties, each with a unique taste. They are naturally sweet and packed with essential nutrients, making them a healthy snack. Rich in vitamins like Vitamin C and fiber, apples help boost immunity and improve digestion. Their crunchy texture and juicy flavor make them refreshing and satisfying to eat. Eating apples regularly can support heart health and provide a natural source of energy.
