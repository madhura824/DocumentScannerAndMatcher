A computer is an electronic device that processes data, performs calculations, and executes instructions to complete tasks.
It consists of hardware (CPU, memory, storage) and software (operating system, applications) to function efficiently.
Computers come in various forms, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and supercomputers, each designed for specific purposes.
They are used in education, business, healthcare, entertainment, and scientific research, making them an essential part of modern life.
The evolution of computers, from vacuum tubes to AI-powered quantum computing, continues to revolutionize technology and innovation.
