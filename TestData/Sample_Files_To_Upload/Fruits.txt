FRUITS:

Fruits are naturally sweet and packed with essential vitamins and minerals, making them a great addition to a healthy diet. They come in a variety of colors, from vibrant red apples to bright yellow bananas, each offering unique nutrients. Rich in vitamins like Vitamin C, A, and antioxidants, fruits help boost immunity and promote overall well-being. Many fruits have high water content, keeping the body hydrated and aiding digestion. Their natural sugars provide a quick energy boost while being a healthier alternative to processed sweets. Apple, mango, orange are types of fruits.
