ORANGE AS A FRUIT:

Oranges are juicy and refreshing fruits known for their bright orange color and citrusy sweetness. They are rich in Vitamin C, which helps boost immunity and keeps the skin healthy. Oranges have a high water content, keeping the body hydrated and aiding digestion. Their tangy flavor makes them a delicious snack, whether eaten fresh or as juice. Regular consumption of oranges supports heart health and provides essential antioxidants.
